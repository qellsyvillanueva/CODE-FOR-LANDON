Check-In & Check-Out Log

How To Check-In Guests:

Choose a room for a guest to check-in to. Type the guests name into the text box. Click on Check-In.

How To Check-Out Guests:

Look for the person that want's to check out. Press the up arrow in the checkout box and go to the number that the name corresponds to. Click on Check-Out
